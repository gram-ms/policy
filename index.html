Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this application collects, uses, stores, and shares information obtained through third-party APIs (such as Meta for Facebook, Instagram, etc.) and user-configured integrations.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the execution of user-configured automations. This may include page IDs, access tokens, and public content from connected accounts, as authorized by the user.

2. Use of Data

The data is used exclusively to execute and manage the user's workflows and integrations, such as publishing content, reading metrics, or responding to webhooks, according to the permissions granted by the user.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data may be sent to third-party services only when explicitly configured by the user within their workflows (e.g., sending a file to a cloud storage service).

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored only as long as necessary for the application's functionality. The user is responsible for the retention and deletion of data within their own environment and connected services.

5. Security

We recommend that users secure their environment and credentials (e.g., using strong passwords, access control, and secure connections). The application does not have access to third-party credentials other than those explicitly provided for an integration.

6. Permissions Requested

The permissions requested (e.g., business_management, pages_read_engagement, instagram_basic, etc.) are used solely for the operations described above and to enable the application's features.
